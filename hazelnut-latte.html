Hazelnut Latte

Recipe & Details

Our limited‑edition Hazelnut Latte is crafted using single‑origin espresso, steamed milk, and house hazelnut syrup. Nutty, aromatic, and perfectly balanced.

Ingredients:

  • 2 shots espresso
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tbsp hazelnut syrup
  • Whipped cream & toasted hazelnuts (optional)

Directions:

  1. Brew espresso shots.
  2. Stir in hazelnut syrup.
  3. Froth milk and pour over espresso.
  4. Top with cream & nuts.
